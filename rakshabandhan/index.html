Festival Traditions

Tying the Rakhi

The sister performs an aarti (prayer ritual) for her brother and ties the sacred rakhi thread on his wrist.

Gift Giving

The brother gives gifts to his sister as a token of love and appreciation for the rakhi.

Sweet Exchange

Siblings exchange sweets, with the sister feeding the brother first, and then vice versa.
